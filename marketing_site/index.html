What makes FraudLens different

Most tools stop at “flag a transaction”. FraudLens delivers the entire incident lifecycle: decision, compliance drafts, and evidence integrity—built for IT teams and exec users.

Autonomous Scribe

Generate RBI (FMR‑style), CERT‑In, and Executive Summary reports from a single incident context.

  • India-first report formats
  • Recipient routing (IT/Compliance vs Leadership)
  • Draft-first to avoid accidental regulator sends

Chronos evidence anchoring

Every report artifact can be stored as a PDF with a SHA‑256 hash for later verification.

  • GCS storage + verifiable hashes
  • Open stored copy from the dashboard
  • Audit trail ready for disputes

Executive dashboards that adapt

Role/persona dashboards with a widget library so each stakeholder sees what matters.

  • CEO/CFO/CRO/CISO personas
  • NCRP + CERT‑In pipeline metrics
  • Trends, top risky VPAs, severity mix

Security and auditability

Tamper-evident evidence

Artifacts are stored as PDFs and referenced with SHA‑256. You can later re-compute and verify integrity.

Role-based access

Separate IT and Executive views. Keep sensitive investigation actions with IT, and high-level outcomes with leadership.

Draft-first compliance

Reports are generated as drafts and routed to internal reviewers—reducing accidental external disclosures.

Cloud-native deployment

Static frontends on CDN + private evidence bucket with API-mediated read. Backend services on Cloud Run.
