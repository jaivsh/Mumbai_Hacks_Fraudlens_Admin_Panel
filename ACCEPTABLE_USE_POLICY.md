# FraudLens — Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) (Template)

**Last updated:** 2026-04-03  
This AUP applies to use of FraudLens (dashboard, APIs, report generation, evidence services, and assistant chatbot).

---

## 1. Prohibited uses
You must not use the Service to:
- violate laws or regulations (including cybercrime, privacy, and financial regulations);
- access data without authorization or exceed your permitted scope;
- attempt to bypass security controls, authentication, or rate limits;
- upload malware or exploit vulnerabilities;
- perform denial-of-service, scraping, or abusive automated traffic beyond agreed limits;
- generate or distribute unlawful, harmful, or deceptive content;
- submit or facilitate fraudulent regulator filings without review and authorization.

---

## 2. Data handling
- Do not upload credentials, secrets, or unnecessary sensitive data.
- Only upload data you have the right to process and share.

---

## 3. Rate limits and fair use
Provider may enforce rate limits and quotas to protect system stability. Repeated violations may result in suspension.

---

## 4. Enforcement
Provider may suspend or terminate access for violations of this AUP, security risk, or legal compliance requirements.
